## Troubleshooting

**Black screen?**
- Check browser console (F12)
- Try the debug page: `test-simple.html`

**No voice?**
- Use Chrome/Edge (best support)
- Check microphone permissions

**No hand tracking?**
- Camera must be allowed
- Face the camera with good lighting
