# 🎮 HELIOS Interactive Earth - Final Controls

## ✅ Complete Control Scheme

### **ONE HAND**

#### **🤏 Pinch (Thumb + Index)**
Pick up Earth and move it around. Release to drop with gravity.

#### **✊ Fist/Grab**
Alternative to pinch - make a fist to grab Earth. Open hand to drop.

---

### **TWO HANDS (Both Pinching)**

When you pinch BOTH hands (thumb+index on each hand), you can:

#### **🔄 Rotate** 
Move your hands left/right/up/down to rotate Earth
- Horizontal movement → Horizontal rotation
- Vertical movement → Vertical rotation
- **500x sensitivity** - tiny movements = dramatic spin!

#### **📏 Scale**
Move your hands apart/together to change Earth size
- Hands apart → Earth grows bigger (max 4x)
- Hands together → Earth shrinks smaller (min 0.5x)
- Smooth scaling from small to huge!

#### **🎯 BOTH AT ONCE!**
You can rotate AND scale simultaneously:
- Move hands left while spreading them apart → Rotate + Grow
- Move hands right while bringing together → Rotate + Shrink
- Full control in all dimensions!

---

### **TWO HANDS (Open/Not Pinching)**

🙌 **Nothing happens** - You must pinch both hands to activate rotation and scaling

---
