## 🚨 Common Demo Issues

### Problem: Hand not tracking
**Solution:** 
- Ensure good lighting
- Center hand in camera view
- Check camera permissions

### Problem: Voice not recognized
**Solution:**
- Speak clearly and at moderate volume
- Check microphone permissions
- Ensure quiet environment

### Problem: Windows not loading
**Solution:**
- Some sites block iframe embedding
- Use suggested sites (YouTube, Google, GitHub)
- Check internet connection

### Problem: Low FPS
**Solution:**
- Close other browser tabs
- Reduce window count
- Mention it's optimizable
